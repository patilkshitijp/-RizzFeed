A Developer’s Perspective on Cinema

Kshitij Patil
April, 1st 2025

Category
Writing, Tips, Others

Hello there! My name is Kshitij Patil, and I’m a frontend developer with a passion for movies. Ever since I started coding, I wanted to merge my love for films with my technical skills. That’s how this project was born!


This is my personal side project, designed to help movie enthusiasts explore and discover films more easily. Whether you're searching for a classic masterpiece or a hidden gem, this platform aims to make your movie journey smoother and more exciting.


Movies have always been a huge part of my life, and I wanted to create something meaningful for fellow movie lovers. This project is just the beginning, and I plan to expand it with new features that enhance the experience.

What’s Next?

I have many ideas for future updates! Here are some planned features: AI-based movie recommendations Trending films and personalized watchlists Interactive discussions and user reviews

This is just the start, and I’m excited to see where it goes. Stay tuned for more updates!
